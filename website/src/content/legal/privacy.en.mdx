_Last updated: December 2025_

## Your privacy is our priority

At Pelvisana, we understand pelvic health is intimate and personal. That’s why we designed the app with privacy as a core principle.

## Data we do NOT collect

- We do not collect personally identifiable data
- We do not store information on external servers
- We do not use third‑party trackers
- We do not share any data with third parties
- We do not require creating an account

## Local storage

All app data (your progress, assessments, and preferences) is stored exclusively on your device. This means:

- You have full control over your data
- You can delete all data at any time
- The app can work fully offline

## Anonymous use

Pelvisana is designed to work anonymously. You do not need to provide your name, email, or any other personal data to use the app.

## Contact

If you have questions about our privacy policy, contact: [pelvisana@onmedic.org](mailto:pelvisana@onmedic.org)
